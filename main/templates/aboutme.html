What I'm currently Learning and working with

I am a student at the Coding Dojo Academy, where I am learning 3 stacks (Python, Java, React) and how to code and solve algorithms along with 11 other students. My advice to you if you are planning to do this: have a good plan and research about it before attending. Follow what you love and not what everyone is doing. If you have passion and curiosity for websites and software, and you have the time, I highly recommend Coding Dojo. Their material is rich with information, but the only issue is with time. Manage your time and ask for help whenever you need it. Keep it civil. I did a year of research before making my decision. It's never too late.
